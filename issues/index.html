Where Cammie Stands

On The Issues

The Probate Court plays a critical role in the lives of individuals and families across Lexington County. During some of life's most important and often difficult moments, people rely on the court for clarity, guidance, and support.

Cammie Campbell is committed to delivering a Probate Court that is efficient, transparent, and centered on the people it serves.

Through her work within the Probate Court, Cammie has developed a deep, firsthand understanding of how the system operates — its processes, its people, and the real-life impact it has on those who depend on it. She has seen what works well and where there are opportunities to improve.

As Probate Judge, she will build on that experience to strengthen the court's operations, support its staff, and ensure the highest level of service for the community.

A Commitment

Efficient & Modern Court Operations

The Probate Court should operate in a way that is organized, timely, and responsive to the needs of the community. Delays and inefficiencies only add stress during already difficult situations.

Cammie is committed to improving court operations by increasing administrative efficiency, streamlining estate filings, and ensuring matters are handled in a timely and structured manner. She also recognizes the growing need for modern tools that better serve both the public and the professionals who rely on the court every day.

By implementing secure, digital systems—similar to those used in many other counties—the Probate Court can provide approved access to records for attorneys and title abstractors, while still maintaining the integrity and accessibility of public records. These improvements will reduce unnecessary delays, improve workflow, and create a more efficient experience for everyone involved.

  • Streamline estate filings to reduce delays and improve processing times
  • Improve administrative efficiency across all areas of the court
  • Ensure timely handling of cases and filings
  • Implement secure digital access systems for attorneys, title abstractors, interested parties, and approved professionals
  • Modernize operations to better serve a growing Lexington County
A Priority

Clear Communication & Public Access

For many individuals, navigating the probate process can feel overwhelming and uncertain. Clear, consistent communication is essential to helping people understand what to expect and how to move forward.

Cammie will prioritize improving communication between court staff and the public, ensuring individuals receive accurate information, timely updates, and a clear understanding of the process. She will also work with county officials to explore secure, modern solutions that improve access to estate records and filings.

  • Provide clear, consistent communication throughout the probate process
  • Ensure individuals receive timely updates and accurate information
  • Improve accessibility to court services for families and professionals
  • Explore secure digital access to estate records and filings
An Investment

Professional, Well-Trained Staff

A strong and effective court depends on a knowledgeable, professional, and well-supported team.

Cammie is committed to investing in updated training and ongoing development for court staff, ensuring they are equipped with the tools and knowledge needed to serve the public with professionalism and consistency. She believes in empowering her team to perform at a high level and take pride in the service they provide.

  • Invest in updated training and continued education for staff
  • Maintain high standards of professionalism and accountability
  • Equip staff with the tools and resources needed to succeed
  • Foster a culture of respect, teamwork, and service
A Service

Compassionate & Respectful

Probate Court is not just about legal processes — it is about people. Many who come before the court are navigating deeply personal and emotional circumstances.

Cammie believes the court should be a place where individuals feel supported, respected, and treated with dignity. She is committed to fostering an environment where compassion and professionalism guide every interaction.

  • Treat every individual with dignity, respect, and understanding
  • Create a supportive and welcoming court environment
  • Ensure the court experience is guided by compassion and care
  • Put people first in every decision and interaction
A Commitment to Service

Building a Better Probate Court for Lexington County

Cammie Campbell is not a career politician — she is stepping forward to serve because she believes the Probate Court can and should work better for the people of Lexington County.

With firsthand experience, a clear vision for improvement, and a commitment to doing what is right, she will work to build a court system that is efficient, accessible, and grounded in integrity.
