Cammie Campbell Announces Candidacy for Probate Judge

Announcement highlights commitment to service, firsthand experience within the court, and a vision for a more efficient, accessible, and compassionate Probate Court

Date
Tuesday, May 5, 2026
Location
Lexington, SC
Press Contact
team@cammiecampbell.com

Lexington, SC — Today, Cammie Campbell announced her candidacy for Lexington County Probate Judge, stepping forward with a commitment to strengthen the court through thoughtful leadership, improved efficiency, and a focus on serving individuals and families during life's most important moments.

Campbell, who currently works within the Lexington County Probate Court, brings firsthand knowledge of the court's operations, its responsibilities, and the critical role it plays in the community. Her decision to run comes from a desire to build upon that experience and further enhance how the court serves the public.

"Most people don't think about the Probate Court until they need it. And when they do, it's often during one of the most difficult moments in their lives. In those moments, people deserve clarity, professionalism, and a system that works the way it should."

Cammie Campbell

Campbell said her experience inside the court has given her a unique perspective on both its strengths and the opportunities to improve.

"I've had the privilege of seeing firsthand how much this court matters — not just in process, but in people's lives. I've also seen where small, thoughtful improvements can make a meaningful difference for families navigating difficult situations."

Cammie Campbell

As Probate Judge, Campbell said she will focus on several key priorities:

  • Improving administrative efficiency and streamlining estate filings to reduce delays
  • Strengthening communication between court staff and the public
  • Supporting ongoing training and development for court staff
  • Exploring secure, modern solutions to improve access to information
  • Ensuring every individual is treated with dignity, respect, and care

Campbell emphasized that her campaign is rooted in service, not politics.

"I am not a career politician. This decision is not about politics — it's about serving people and making sure the Probate Court reflects the values of this community."

Cammie Campbell

In addition to her professional experience, Campbell said her decision to run was shaped by her life at home and her commitment to Lexington County.

"This is where my husband and I are raising our family, and we care deeply about this community. I believe in doing what is right, serving others, and ensuring that our local institutions reflect the integrity and responsibility that make Lexington County such a special place."

Cammie Campbell

Campbell said she looks forward to connecting with voters in the coming months and sharing her vision for the future of the Probate Court.

For more information about Cammie and her campaign for Lexington County Probate Judge, please visit www.cammiecampbell.com.
