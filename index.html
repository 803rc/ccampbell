Efficient & Modern Court Operations

About Cammie

Ready to Serve Lexington County

Cammie Campbell brings firsthand experience inside the Probate Court and a clear vision for how it can serve the families of Lexington County more effectively.

She is committed to a court that is efficient, transparent, and centered on the people it serves — because behind every filing is a family navigating one of life's most important moments.

"The Probate Court is about people — not just process. Every family that walks through our doors deserves dignity, clarity, and care." — Cammie Campbell
